Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Visit our Courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click the enrollment button. You'll be guided through the registration process.

Are there prerequisites for courses?

Most beginner courses have no prerequisites. Advanced courses may require basic programming knowledge, which is clearly stated in the course description.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your portfolio or resume.

What if I need help during a course?

We provide multiple support channels including instructor office hours, peer discussion forums, and technical support via email.
